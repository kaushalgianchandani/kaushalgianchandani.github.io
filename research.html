Research Focus

I combine theory and numerical modeling to understand how ocean circulation shapes climate across a wide range of planetary environments. My north star is to uncover the mechanisms that organize large-scale circulation, regulate heat and tracer transport, and drive nontrivial climate transitions on Earth and beyond.
